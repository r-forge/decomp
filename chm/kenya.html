kenya(decomp)R Documentation

Sample dataset

Description

A sample dataset, based on a DHS household survey of Kenya (2003).

Usage

kenya

Format

A data frame with 79440 observations on the following 6 variables.

stunting
a numeric vector; a measure of the subject's stunting
wealth
a numeric vector; a measure of the household's wealth
popweight
a numeric vector, containing sample weight
meducY
a numeric vector; the subject's mother's education in years
sex
a factor with levels male female
urban
a factor with levels 0 1; with 0: rural and 1: urban

Source

http://www.measuredhs.com

Examples


data(kenya)

str(kenya)
